Project door lock
--------------------------------------------------------------

Elements:

Numerical keypad 3*4 --- pins: 
	P1 P2 P3 P4 P5 P6 P7
digital --	9   8   7    6   5   4   3

Lcd connected with I2C konverter:
	Scl --- A5
	Sda --- A4
	Vcc --- 5V
	Gnd --- Gnd

PIR:
	Vcc --- 5V
	Out --- 10
	Gnd --- Gnd

Servo:
	Control pin --- 2
	Vcc --- 5V
	Gnd --- Gnd

Microswitch:
	C (1) --- Gnd
	No (2) --- 11
